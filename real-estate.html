OAK

OAK, our current signature project, is an exciting new type of development — one that will change the way life is lived and work is done in Oklahoma

The 200-acre development features 250,000 sf Class ‘A’ Office, 60,000 sf Loft Office, 300 Residential units, 7,000 sf Community Green and a 140 key Lifestyle Hotel.

Veritas Development was founded in 2004 to provide development services for retail and restaurant tenants. From navigating northern California entitlement challenges to identifying the best locations for tenants in the Midwest, we provide clients a trusted process and best-in-class design.
