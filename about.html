About Us

From agricultural origins in the wheat belt a century ago to blending preservation with innovative community-strengthening real estate projects today, we’re driven by integrity, preservation, and honesty in everything we do.

Ryan McNeil

Ryan, an Oklahoma City-area native, founded Veritas Development in 2004. Veritas began as a retail development company and has since developed or acquired more than 425,000 square feet of Class ‘A’ retail, office and hospitality space. He graduated with a B.A. in Finance from Bob Jones University in Greenville, South Carolina. The McNeill family own and operate banking, oil & gas and ranching interests in Oklahoma, Texas, New Mexico and Colorado. Ryan is a board member of Bank of the West. He and his wife have four children and are active with ministries to children in their local church.

Chris George

Chris oversees construction for Veritas Development. A Missouri native, Chris has 25 years of experience in diferent aspects of construction, design and development. Chris joined Veritas after overseeing a luxury, planned community at Grand Lake. A B.S. in Pre-Med and Minor in Bible, Chris, his wife and two children serve in their church while partnering with global organizations in charitable resourcing and strategic consulting.

Ashlyn McCorkle

Ashlyn is a Tulsa native and lifeflong Oklahoman. She leads the property management functions for Veritas Development. Ashlyn is a graduate from the University of Oklahoma with degrees in both General Management and Entrepreneurship and Venture Management. She currently serves on the board of her sorority’s alumnae group for Northeastern Oklahoma and volunteers at several local charitable organizations
